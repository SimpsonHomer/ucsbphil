Research Abstract

My interests in philosophy are broad, but I mostly find myself working within philosophy of language, logic, and metaphysics. Currently, I’m working on the distinction between semantics and pragmatics, namely, on what a semantic theory can tell us about language. In trying to answer this question I am looking at specific questions relating to natural language including what the objects of a semantic theory are, whether we can formally represent demonstratives, variable binding, and tests for determining whether an instance of contextual influence on communication arises from a context sensitive expression or through a pragmatic process.
