Research Abstract

I am currently interested in questions concerning the nature of mental representation and causation and explanation. One such question is how can perceptual experiences purport to be revelatory of the world. Another question is in virtue of what do answers to “how” and “why” questions bear explanatory relevance and value. Yet another question is whether the context-relativity of causal claims undermines their objectivity.
