Philip Atkins, PhD

Areas of Study

  • Philosophy of Language
  • Philosophy of Mind
  • Epistemology

Research Abstract

My areas of interest are the philosophy of language, the philosophy of mind, and epistemology. I have recently worked on problems concerning the contingent a priori, de re belief, and perceptual justification. I am currently working on the semantics of belief reports.
