Research Abstract

I got my B.A. at SUNY Geneseo, where I majored in Philosophy and minored in Anthropology. After that I studied Philosophy for two years at SUNY Buffalo, where I focused primarily on philosophical semantics. Since arriving at UCSB, I’ve become increasingly interested in the metaphysics of mind, philosophy of perception, and action theory. Currently I am thinking about two topics: the metaphysics of color and the semantics of “intends” and related phrases.
