Research Abstract

I’m currently most interested in theories of government: what justifies the authority of government, if anything? I believe that once we get straight on that issue, questions of good policy are much more readily answered.

Of course, the even deeper question is the nature of morality. We must know what makes things right or wrong, in order to give a well-grounded account of government.
