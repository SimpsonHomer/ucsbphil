Staff Support for Philosophy

The South Hall Administrative Support Center (SASC) provides administrative services for the departments of English, ESL, Linguistics, Philosophy, and the Writing Program. It includes Academic Services and Student Services units.

  • 3431 and 3432 South Hall

  • Hours: 9:00–noon, 1:00–4:00 PM Monday–Friday

  • Student Advising Hours: 9:00–11:00 AM, 1:30–3:00 PM

  • Main Info lines: 805-893-7488 or 805-893-7489

  • Fax: 805-893-7492 or 805-893-7491
