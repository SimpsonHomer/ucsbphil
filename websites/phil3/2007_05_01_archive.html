Thursday, May 24, 2007

Reminder, sections for midterm

Here are the sections that you are going to be tested on:

5.1 Categorical statements; standard form, stylistic variants. Know how to put English into categorical statements. Know how to give a scheme of abbreviation.
5.3 Immediate inferences w/ categorical statements: conversion, obversion, contraposition. I will attach the rules if you get a problem like this, but you must know how to use them.
6.1 Standard form syllogisms,
6.2 Venn diagrams and categorical statements,
6.3 Venn and categorical syllogisms
6.5, 6.6 Enthymemes, Sorites and removing term complements. There will be one sorites problem, and you might have to remove a term compliment.
7.1 Symbolizing arguments, logical operators. Know how to put English sentences into symbols.
7.2 Truth tables for logical operators. Must know these by heart.
7.3 Truth tables for validity of arguments
7.4 Abbreviated truth tables for validity of arguments
7.5 Tautology, contradiction, contingency and logical equivalence. Know how to prove this.
